Qualification Details

ICT40120 | CERTIFICATE IV IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (WEB DEVELOPMENT)

Course overview

This qualification reflects the role of individuals who are job ready and competent in a wide range of information and communications technology (ICT) roles and apply a broad range of skills in varied work contexts, using problem solving skills and effective communication with others. This qualification provides an individual with the skills and knowledge to design, develop, build and administer websites using a range of website technologies.
